Lory's Travel Page

This page documents a few of Lory's vacation photos, her favorite sites, also lists the places she would like to travel to next.

Paris, France

Arc du Triomphe at Sunset

  • The Eiffel Tower Rent a bicycle and ride along the Seine River after sunset for the most breathtaking view of this gorgeous structure.
  • Arc du Triomphe Climb all the up the Arc on a clear day for a amazing view of the whole city.
  • The Jewish Quarter Order "the world's best falafel" at L'As du Fallafel.

Brussels, Belgium

Belgian Beer Festival at Grand Place

  • Grand-Place Visit the main city square to experience the gorgeous royal buildings, eat some Belgian waffles, and drink some great Belgian brews.
  • Parc du Cinquantenaire Enjoy a picnic in this large public park celebrating 50 years of Belgian independence.
  • Royal Palace Take a free tour of the Royal Palace and take in its beautiful architecture and oppulance.

Amsterdam, Netherlands

Dutch Row Houses Along a Canal

  • Rijksmuseum Spend the day in the expansive national museum and get your share of Rembrandts and Vermeers, among other famous Dutch artists.
  • Canal Tour Take in the city through a canal tour and learn more about its history.
  • Anne Frank House Take a tour of the Anne Frank house to learn more about Anne and her family's experience during WWII.
